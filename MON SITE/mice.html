Conception

A Destination Management Company (DMC) is a professional services company possessing extensive local knowledge, expertise and resources, specializing in the design and implementation of events, activities, tours, transportation and program logistics.

Every end of August, the MICE Summer Week takes place in the North of Europe. During one week, every evening, in several cities, is held a networking-congress event where differents clients and companies are proposed to meet with a drink. As a producer I was ask to film and create some web material to promote the company and its events. My work consisted in shoot, edit ad animate two main videos and thirty individuals interviews.

Realisation

My goal was to make a web format, giving the biggest FOMO imaginable to everyone will look at it. So I began to shoot firstly the indivduals interviews, to get the best of the daylight while we where waiting for the guests to arrive.

Once done, the easthetic pleasing part came up, how to show the venue in its best angle ? So I tried to shoot differents but coherents sequences to give me the best amplitude during my editing. Back at home I edit those differents videos in a total of full twelve days of works (but spared on two months to keep a fresh view on it).

Lesson n°2

Give the objects the appearance of wanting to be there.

Final product

The final product is two 6 minutes videos of two of the biggest evenings (Ghent & Pullheim) and thirty individuals interviews of the DMC agents. They are used on youtube and as a showcase for their partner on their websites.
